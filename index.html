Vision

We believe that fast-deliveries as well as services like drone-selfies or air-video-footage will soon be available as a service at a lot of places.

So we developed a platform that enables everyone to use their drones to provide a delivery service. With predefined flight-zones it is possible to avoid collisions with static objects like trees and houses.

Features

Collision Avoidance

You can draw different types of zones in which your drones can navigate. They move only inside these borders and avoid collisions with static objects.

Route Calculation

When the system receives an order from a customer it automatically calculates a route through your predefined flight zones.

Compatibility

Our system works with every drone that supports MAVLink Protocol. (Tested with Pixhawk and Ardupilot v3.3)

Customer App

We provide an App (iOS and Android), which every customer can download. It automatically shows your products if the customer is inside your catchment area.

Real-Time

All your drones are controlled over the Internet. Just use our platform to track the movement and actions of your drones in real-time.

Onboard App

Just use our Onboard-App to connect your drones with our platform. It also provides a user-interface which helps you to keep track of the state of your drone.

Getting Started

Create your Organisation

Go to my.helin.ch create your personal account and create an organisation. From now on you can add drones and products to your organisation and share them between different locations and events (Projects).

Create your Project

You are now able to create a project which defines where and what you will provide for your customers. The map will help you to define where you have the base of your drones and in which area you will deliver. You can test all your projects with ease by simulating your drone and your customer-position.

Prepare your Drone

In order to connect your drone with the platform you have mount an Android smartphone on it and connect it with an OTG-Cable or use a Radio-Telemetry-Set. You can download the Helin-Onboard-App here and connect it by typing in the drone token from the drones overview in my.helin.ch. If you wan't to drop your products with a parachute, you can 3D-Print our drop-mechanism.
